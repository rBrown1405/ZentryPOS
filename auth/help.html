Everything you need to know about using Zentry POS

üè¢ For Business Owners

Setting up your business account:

  • Click "New Business / Staff Registration" on the login page
  • Select "Business Owner" to create a new business account
  • Fill in your company information and first property details
  • You'll receive a Business ID for future logins

Managing your establishment:

  • Add and manage multiple properties (restaurants, hotels, retail stores)
  • Create staff accounts and assign roles (Manager, Server, Cashier, Kitchen)
  • Set up menu items, tables, and floor plans
  • Access detailed analytics and reports
  • Configure system settings and permissions

üë• For Staff Members

Joining a business:

  • Ask your manager for a connection code or Staff ID
  • Click "New Business / Staff Registration" on the login page
  • Select "Staff Member" and enter your connection code
  • Complete your profile information

Using the POS system:

  • Use your Staff ID for quick login
  • Access features based on your assigned role
  • Process orders, manage tables, and handle payments
  • View your assigned tasks and responsibilities

üîë Login Types & Troubleshooting

Staff Login:

  • Enter your Staff ID (provided by your manager)
  • Quick access to POS interface
  • Role-based permissions automatically applied

Company Login:

  • Enter your Business ID (received during registration)
  • Full management access to your business
  • Access to admin dashboard and settings

Super Admin Login:

  • System administration access
  • Requires special credentials
  • For system maintenance only

Common Issues:

  • Forgot your ID? Contact your business owner or system administrator
  • Access denied? Check with your manager about your account status
  • Page not loading? Try refreshing or check your internet connection
